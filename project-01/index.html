Welcome to the PT's world, where Healing Meets Movement!

Hey there! I’m Fatima Thawerani, a passionate Physical Therapy student stepping into the amazing world of healing, movement, and recovery. This space is where I share my journey from classroom lessons to real-life experiences as I learn what it truly means to help people move better and live stronger.

About My Journey

Physical therapy isn’t just a career path for me, it’s a calling. Every muscle, joint, and movement tells a story, and I’m here to understand it all. Through my studies, internships, and hands-on practice, I’m discovering how science and compassion work together to change lives.

Here, you’ll find reflections, study tips, therapy insights, and stories that inspire both future PTs and anyone curious about how the body heals through movement.

What You’ll Find Here!

  • Learning & Growth: My notes, study guides, and PT school tips.
  • Clinical Adventures: Real experiences from labs and internships.
  • Mind & Body Connection: Thoughts on wellness, motivation, and balance.
  • Community: A space for students, aspiring PTs, and movement lovers.

Why I Started This Page?

I wanted to create a space that celebrates what being a PT student is all about — the challenges, the victories, the endless learning, and the joy of helping others move freely again.

If you’re a fellow PT student, a future therapist, or just someone who loves learning about the human body — welcome to the PT’s World.
